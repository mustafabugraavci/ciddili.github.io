Scientifics Calculations - Collision and rebound.

FREE

This demo simulates projection physics & collision detection for different shaps & materials. Also this demo provides a physical study of light & sounds effects. In addition to studying several external factors which effects on shape movement "like air resistance & coefficient of friction" objects will also fall "asleep" when thier average movement has gone below a certain threshold. This enables us to skip updating these objects, which will increase the performance of the simulation..
